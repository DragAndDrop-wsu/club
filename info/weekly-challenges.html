DAD's Weekly Programming Challenges

Objective

A series of weekly programming challenges, designed to boost your overall skills in computer science and engineering topics. At the end of each month, DAD will pick a user to proclaim as the winner for that month. The winner is allowed to put being that month's winner on their resume (looks great under the Awards & Honors section).

Winner Selection

Depending on the number of people participating \(\ge 3\) there will be awards for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.

In order of highest to least precedence in what we look for:

  • Number of solutions solved, the higher the level the more weight it carries.
    • This ranking can be calculated through: \(4A + 2I + N\), where A are the amount of advance problems solved, I is the number of intermediate problems solved, and N is the number of novice problems solved.
  • A functioning solution
